About Me

Programmer, Engineer, Musician

Ever since a young age, I have the interest of engineering, whether that be to craft objects or break things apart. After learning that programming and engineering go hand in hand, I delved deeper into programming, learning more about OOP languages, web development, and AI. Now, I use both of those skills to build some robotics projects! Furthermore, I am also an avid musician. I play the Indian drums called the tabla. I have been playing the tabla for more than 12 years and I have given numerous concerts! I also have been playing the viola for the past 6 years and have been in the advanced orchestra levels throughout my school years! After gaining knowledge of both types of music (Indian classical and string), I have really been able to delve deeper into this mind-blowing realm!
